    Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Chuncky Peanut Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon of Olive Oil
  • 1 Chopped Onion
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic, Peeled and Chopped
  • 1 Cup of Fresh Cilantro, Chopped
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 1 Can of Whole Peeled and Tomatoes, Drained
  • 2 Teaspoons of Curry Powder
  • 1 Whole Chicken (Bones and skin cut into pieces)
  • 1 Lemon, Juiced

    Instructions

    Prepare the cooking pot/skillet:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large, heavy skillet over medium heat.

    2. Prepare the cooking pot/skillet:

  2. Stir in the onion, garlic, and bay leaf, and saute until onion is lightly browned.

    3. Cook the chicken:

  3. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels; season with salt and pepper. In a medium pot, heat 2 teaspoons of olive oil on medium-high until hot.Mix tomatoes, curry powder, and salt into the skillet, and continue cooking about 5 minutes.
  4. Mix in the chicken, and cook 15 to 20 minutes, until no longer pink and juices run clear.Stir the peanut butter into the curry, partially cover the pot/skillet and continue cooking for another 20 minutes.

    5. Finish cooking and serve your dish

  5. Reduce skillet heat to low. Mix in lemon juice just before serving.
